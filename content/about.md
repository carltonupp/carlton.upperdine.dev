I’m currently building SaaS products for the rail industry at [3Squared](https://www.3squared.com/), and have previously worked on software for the automotive sector. Commercially, I am experienced in using C# and ASP.NET to build web applications, but in my free time I like to dabble in other languages such as Node.js, Python, Elixir and F#.

Some of my areas of interest within the industry include:

* Cloud computing (mainly Microsoft Azure)
* Distributed Systems
* DevOps
* Open Source Software
* Functional Programming
* Serverless Computing

I am also particularly passionate about making the tech industry accessible to those without a traditional background, and have assisted a few people in learning to code and securing a junior developer position.
